Privacy Policy
Work Chain Bazaar
Last updated: June 21, 2025
Introduction
Work Chain Bazaar ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for job marketplace services.
Information We Collect
Personal Information
• Email Address: For account creation and authentication
• Phone Number: For payment processing and confirmations
• Profile Information: Name and profile picture (optional)
Location Information
• GPS Location: To show nearby jobs and calculate distances
• Location Preferences: To personalize job recommendations
Payment Information
• Payment Data: Processed securely through Razorpay (we do not store payment details)
• Transaction History: For order tracking and support
User Content and Communications
• Job Postings: Job descriptions, images, and requirements you create
• Messages: Communications between job posters and workers
• Reviews and Ratings: Feedback you provide about jobs or workers
Technical Information
• Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
• Usage Data: App features used, session duration, crash reports
• Log Data: Error logs and performance metrics for app improvement
How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
• Provide and maintain our job marketplace service
• Process payments and transactions
• Show relevant nearby job opportunities
• Authenticate your account and prevent fraud
• Improve our app performance and user experience
• Send important notifications about your jobs and account
• Provide customer support
Data Security
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
• Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
• Secure authentication with OTP verification
• Regular security updates and monitoring
• Limited access to personal information
Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to:
• Access: Request a copy of your personal data
• Update: Modify your profile information in the app
• Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
• Location: Turn off location services in device settings
Third-Party Services
Our app integrates with:
• Razorpay: Payment processing
• Google Maps: Location services
• Expo: App development platform
Information Sharing and Disclosure
We may share your information in the following circumstances:
• Job Marketplace Function: Job posters can see basic profile information of applicants
• Service Providers: With Razorpay for payment processing, Google for location services
• Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users
• With Your Consent: Any other sharing only with your explicit permission
Data Retention
We retain your information for the following periods:
• Account Data: Until you delete your account
• Transaction Records: For 7 years (legal requirement)
• Job Postings: Until you delete them or account closure
• Log Data: For 90 days maximum
Children's Privacy
Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us to have the information removed.
International Data Transfers
Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including India and the United States, where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
• Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
• Sending an email notification for significant changes
• Displaying an app notification when you next open the app
Your continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
• Email: admin@workchainbazaar.com
• Through the Work Chain Bazaar mobile application
By using Work Chain Bazaar, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
