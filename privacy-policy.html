Privacy Policy
Work Chain Bazaar
Last updated: June 27, 2025
Introduction
Work Chain Bazaar ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with Google Play Store policies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for job marketplace services.
User Consent:
All document collection is completely optional and requires explicit user consent. You can use our basic services without providing any identity documents.
Information We Collect
Required Information (for basic app functionality)
• Email Address: For account creation and authentication
• Phone Number: For OTP verification and job-related communications
• Profile Information: Name and profile picture (optional)
• Location: To show nearby job opportunities (with permission)
Optional Identity Verification (User Consent Required)
Completely Optional:
Identity verification is NOT required to use the app. It only provides a "Verified" badge for enhanced trust between users. You maintain full access to all app features without verification.
Purpose: Identity verification helps build trust in the marketplace and enables higher-value job opportunities. This feature is designed for user safety and fraud prevention.
• Government ID Documents: Aadhaar Card, Driving License, or PAN Card (user chooses which to provide, if any)
• Purpose: Identity verification for enhanced profile trust
• Consent: Explicit opt-in required with clear explanation
• Withdrawal: Can be removed at any time without losing app access
Optional Skill Verification (User Consent Required)
Skill Enhancement:
Upload professional certificates to showcase your qualifications. This is entirely optional and helps you stand out to potential employers.
• Professional Certifications: Trade certificates, educational qualifications
• Experience Documents: Previous work certificates or recommendations
• Training Certificates: Skill development course completions
• Purpose: Showcase skills to potential employers
Payment Information (Only for Premium Features)
• Payment Data: Processed securely through Razorpay (we don't store card details)
• Transaction History: For job payments and fee tracking
• Bank Details: Only for receiving job payments (optional)
User-Generated Content
• Job Postings: Descriptions, images, and requirements you create
• Messages: Communications between users (encrypted)
• Reviews: Feedback and ratings you provide
Technical Information
• Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version
• Usage Analytics: App features used, crash reports (anonymized)
• Log Data: Error logs for app improvement only
How We Use Your Information
We use your information solely for the following purposes:
• Core Service: Connecting job seekers with employers
• User Authentication: Secure login and account management
• Job Matching: Show relevant opportunities based on location and skills
• Payment Processing: Handle job payments securely
• Trust & Safety: Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
• User Support: Respond to queries and resolve disputes
• App Improvement: Fix bugs and enhance user experience
Document Verification Use:
• Display "Verified" or "Skilled" badges on profiles (with user consent)
• Enable access to higher-value job opportunities
• Comply with legal requirements for financial transactions above certain limits
• Prevent fraud and maintain platform integrity
User Control and Consent
Google Play Compliance:
We fully comply with Google Play's User Data Policy, including prominent disclosure, user control, and limited use of sensitive data.
Clear Consent Process
• Explicit Consent: Clear explanation before any document upload
• Optional Nature: Prominently displayed that verification is not mandatory
• Purpose Explanation: Clear description of why documents are needed
• Benefits Outlined: What users gain from verification
User Rights
• View Documents: See all uploaded documents in your profile
• Delete Anytime: Remove documents without losing app access
• Download Copies: Request copies of your data
• Account Deletion: Complete removal of all data and documents
• Consent Withdrawal: Opt out of verification at any time
Data Security and Protection
Enterprise-Grade Security:
All sensitive documents are protected with bank-level encryption and security measures that exceed industry standards.
Security measures include:
• End-to-End Encryption: All documents encrypted during upload and storage
• Secure Cloud Storage: Enterprise-grade Cloudinary with SOC 2 compliance
• Access Controls: Only authorized verification staff can access documents
• Audit Logs: All access tracked and monitored
• Regular Security Audits: Third-party security assessments
• HTTPS/TLS: All data transmission encrypted
• Auto-Deletion: Documents removed when no longer needed
Data Sharing and Disclosure
Limited Sharing:
We NEVER share your actual documents with other users. Only verification status (verified/unverified badge) is visible to others.
Information sharing is limited to:
• Public Profile Info: Name, skills, verification badges (with consent)
• Service Providers: Razorpay for payments, Cloudinary for secure storage
• Legal Compliance: When required by law or court order
• Emergency Situations: To prevent harm or fraud
We DO NOT share:
• Your actual identity documents with anyone
• Personal information for marketing purposes
• Data with third parties for advertising
• Information with employers beyond basic profile details
Third-Party Services
We only integrate with verified, secure service providers:
• Razorpay: PCI DSS compliant payment processing
• Google Maps: Location services (with permission)
• Cloudinary: SOC 2 compliant secure document storage
• Expo/React Native: App development framework
Data Retention
We retain information only as long as necessary:
• Profile Data: Until account deletion
• Identity Documents: Deleted immediately upon user request or account closure
• Financial Records: 7 years (legal requirement in India)
• Chat Messages: 1 year or until deletion
• Job Postings: Until removed by user
• Analytics Data: 90 days maximum (anonymized)
Legal Compliance
We comply with applicable laws while minimizing data collection:
• Google Play Policy: Full compliance with user data policies
• IT Act 2000 (India): Data protection and privacy rules
• KYC Requirements: Only for high-value transactions (₹50,000+)
• Labour Laws: Worker protection and rights
• Consumer Protection: Fair marketplace practices
Age Restrictions
Our service is intended for users 18 years and older, in compliance with legal working age requirements in India. We do not knowingly collect information from minors under 18.
International Users
While primarily serving Indian users, we ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers to our secure service providers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
Changes to This Policy
We will notify you of significant changes through:
• In-app notification
• Email notification (for document-related changes)
• 30-day advance notice for material changes
• Option to withdraw consent for new uses
Contact Us
For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:
• Email: privacy@workchainbazaar.com
• Response time: Within 48 hours
